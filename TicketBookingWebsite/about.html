About Us

Welcome toCottonCandyLand!


Since 1960, CottonCandyLand has been a cherished destination for children and families in the heart of London.


What We Offer

Exciting Rides: From gentle carousels for the little ones to thrilling roller coasters for the more adventurous, there's something for everyone.
Interactive Play Areas: Engaging and educational play zones where kids can explore and discover.
Entertainment Shows: Captivating performances and events that bring stories and characters to life.
Delicious Treats: A variety of food stalls offering everything from classic cotton candy to healthy snacks.


Our Values
At CottonCandyLand, we are committed to:

Safety: Ensuring a safe and secure environment for all our visitors.
Inclusivity: Creating a welcoming space where every child feels valued and included.
Sustainability: Implementing eco-friendly practices to preserve our environment for future generations.
Community: Being an active and positive presence in the local community.
